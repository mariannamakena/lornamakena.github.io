Flash Appeal & Response Plan

Summary

The Flash Appeal & Response Plan project aims to streamline the process of responding to humanitarian crises by providing a centralized platform for coordinating flash appeals. It enables organizations to quickly assess needs, allocate resources, and track response efforts in real-time.

This project includes:

  • People in Need: Visualizes the total number of individuals affected by the crisis and requiring assistance.
  • People Targeted: Shows the subset of people who are prioritized to receive aid based on available resources.
  • Clusters: Displays sectoral groups (such as Health, Shelter, Food Security) coordinating the response.
  • Funding Status: Illustrates the funding requirements versus funds received for the appeal.
  • Geographical Coverage: Maps affected areas and locations of ongoing interventions.
