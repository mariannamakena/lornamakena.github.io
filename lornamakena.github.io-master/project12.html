Flood Affected Farms in Busia

Summary

This map illustrates the farms in Busia County, Kenya, that have been affected by flooding. The data highlights the regions near River Nzoia where agricultural activities have been significantly impacted.

Purpose

The purpose of this map is to visually represent the extent of flood-affected farms in Busia County, Kenya, specifically those located near the River Nzoia. This visualization serves multiple objectives:

  • Awareness and Communication: Communicate the scale and impact of flooding on agricultural land to the public, local communities, and stakeholders.
  • Disaster Management and Planning: Aid government agencies, non-governmental organizations, and disaster management teams in planning and implementing effective flood mitigation and response strategies.
  • Resource Allocation: Support decision-makers in allocating resources for emergency relief, rehabilitation, and long-term development projects aimed at improving flood resilience in the region.
  • Research and Analysis: Serve as a valuable tool for researchers and analysts studying the patterns and causes of flooding.
  • Agricultural Planning: Help farmers and agricultural planners identify high-risk areas and develop strategies to minimize crop loss and soil erosion due to future flooding events.

Methods

The map was created using satellite imagery and GIS data to identify the areas affected by flooding. The boundaries of the farms were overlaid on the satellite image to highlight the specific regions impacted by floodwaters.

Findings

The analysis revealed that a significant portion of farmland near River Nzoia is susceptible to flooding. The highlighted areas on the map represent the most affected regions, which require immediate attention for flood mitigation and disaster management efforts.
